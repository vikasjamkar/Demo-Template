Situation

A Financial Sponsor owned global institution seeking to expand EBITDA 3- fold in 3-4 years

Complication

  • Limited product footprint
  • Operations in over 50 countries with varying regulatory burdens
  • Global management with limited previous experience
  • Limited commercial / distribution Partnerships

Resolution

Wilson Dichiara supported client in:

  • Designing the overall growth projects and developed funding requirements
  • Developed business case and secured funding from the Board
  • Designed delivery model and rolled- out teams to ensure execution of growth projects
  • Designed commercial products and identified commercial partners
  • Supported negotiations with commercial partners
